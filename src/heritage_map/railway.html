Adelaide Railway Station

1863 State Library of South Australia / 2023 Alexander Cocks

The original station, a charming limestone structure, was established in 1856 and was the first Government-owned and steam-operated railway in the British Empire.

Today's station, famous for its beautiful Art Deco aesthetic, opened to the public in 1928 and serves over 12-million people per year!
