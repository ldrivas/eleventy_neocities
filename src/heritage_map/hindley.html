Hindley Street

1912 State Library of South Australia / 2023 Alexander Cocks

This view is of considerable activity, as numerous people go about their daily business. A young family, children in hand, cross King William Street, while horse and cart trot the dusty roads carrying cargo and passengers.

Despite the presence of a few contemporary ‘sight-blocking’ structures today, a number of historic buildings continue to stand on Hindley Street.

One of these is the former Miller Anderson’s department store, once one of Australia’s oldest stores (established 1839, receivership in 1988), who accommodated the 1911-built bell-domed corner structure seen at the right. It is currently occupied by…
