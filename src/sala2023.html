SALA 2023

"Chaos and Cosmos"

I am thrilled to be part of this exciting project. Please mark your calendars for an unforgettable experience at The Exeter, where our exhibition will take place from the 3rd to the 31st of August. The festivities kick off with an opening night on the 3rd, complete with delectable nibbles and refreshing drinks. Our exhibition will showcase the incredible works of nine local artists, including Shani Engelbrecht, Charlotte Prior (of "Nipslip"), Hollis Souter, Olivia Bailetti, and Finn Murabito. Prepare to immerse yourself in a captivating fusion of chaos and cosmos, where artistic expression reigns supreme.
